Palace Foods

Operating also as Patty Palace was founded in Canada in 1977. The Plant is located in Scarborough Ontario and is FEDERALLY INSPECTED, USDA and HACCP recognized.

Palace Foods Products are presently sold in Major Supermarkets, Convenience Stores, Club Stores and in Food Service in Canada coast to coast as well as in the United States.

The Products that Palace Foods presently produces are mainly filled pastries. The fill varieties include:

  • Meats
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Fruit

A full variety of different pastries are available. Different shapes can be produced with the molds we have at Palace Foods.
